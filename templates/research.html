Research

During the event, the HackIllinois Research Team will be performing some in-flight experiments to better understand how participants experience HackIllinois and hackathons in general.

The study consists of two surveys: one that opens at the beginning of the event and one that will open near the end.

While we would appreciate your input, please know that you are not required to complete the survey and the answers will in no way affect your hackathon submissions. Your identity will be kept confidential to the research team and HackIllinois staff.

Confidentiality

We will keep your information confidential by storing your response with a unique string. This string is a hash of your email other secret information, so your data will not be directly connected to your personal information from the HackIllinois profile.
